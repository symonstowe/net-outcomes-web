What Net Outcomes Can Analyze

Season Player Reports

Single-player breakdowns can package offensive talent, defensive talent, finishing, playmaking, rush defence, chance suppression, usage context, percentile rankings, and static 5v5 shot maps into one shareable scorecard.

Lineup and Unit Construction

We can build even-strength lines, defence pairs, power-play units, and penalty-kill groups with role-aware deployment logic, projected workload balancing, and side-specific fit where the roster supports it.

Game Scorecards

Pregame or postgame scorecards can combine shot maps, matchup notes, player usage, expected goals flow, line-level results, and the most important drivers from the game into a compact PDF that is easy to review.

Special Teams Diagnostics

Power play and penalty kill reports can split offensive creation, finishing, defensive suppression, PK exposure, penalty impact, goalie influence, and unit usage so it is clear what is driving results.

Situation and Matchup Splits

We can isolate 5v5, 5v4, 4v5, late-game, score-state, recent-form, QoC, QoT, and opponent-style effects to show where a player or team is thriving, surviving, or being miscast.

Custom Research Requests

Specific players, pairs, lines, opponents, roster decisions, trade targets, deployment questions, and in-game breakdowns can all be turned into focused reports when there is a clear hockey question to answer.
